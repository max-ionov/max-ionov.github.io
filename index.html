I am a PhD student at the Applied Computational Linguistics group at the Goethe University Frankfurt. Currently I am involved in the project Linked Open Dictionaries under the supervision of Prof. Dr. Christian Chiarcos.

I am also a lecturer at the department of Theoretical and Applied Linguistics at the Moscow State University.

My main research interests are:

  • Linked data and Semantic Web
  • Computational aid of empirical linguistic research
  • Anaphora and coreference resolution
  • Discourse processing
